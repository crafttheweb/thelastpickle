Who We Are

We work with clients to deliver and improve Apache Cassandra based solutions.

To do that we employ active members of the Cassandra community. People who spend their time understanding how Cassandra works and how others are using it. People who see the Cassandra ecosystem and community around it as an opportunity for professional and personal growth. We feel these are the sort of people who can best communicate their knowledge and enthusiasm to clients.

If this sounds like you then we'd love to hear from you.
